Treasure Hunt Game

How to play:

1) Enter your Name before clicking the “Start Digging” Button.

1) Press the “Start Digging” button below, when you’re ready

2) The objective is to find the treasure chest buried among one of the three islands.

3) You start off with 1 shovel, and each shovel has 1 use. If you have zero remaining shovels the game is over.

4) Click one of the following 3 islands to dig for treasure. If you find the buried treasure, YOU WIN!!!

5) To play again, click the “Play Again?” Button at the bottom.
