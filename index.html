Treasure Hunt Game

How to play :

1) Please enter your name first, before clicking the “Start Digging” button to play.

2) Press the “Start Digging” button below, once you entered your name.

3) The objective is to find the treasure chest buried among one of the three islands.

4) You start off with 1 shovel, and each shovel has 1 use. If you have zero shovels left the game is over.

5) To Win the game, click on one of the following 3 islands to dig for treasure. If you find the buried treasure, YOU WIN!!!.

6) To play again, click the “Play Again?” button at the bottom to restart the game.
