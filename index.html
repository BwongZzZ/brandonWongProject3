Treasure Hunt Game

How to play :

1) Please enter your name first, before clicking the “Start Digging” button to play.

2) The objective is to find the treasure chest buried among one of the three islands (Please find island images below).

3) You start off with one shovel, and each shovel has one dig use. If you have zero shovels left the game is over.

4) Click on one of the following three islands to dig for treasure. If you find the buried treasure, YOU WIN!!!.

5) To play again, click the “Play Again?” button at the bottom to restart the game. Good luck!!! Happy Treasure Hunting!!!
